<b>Offline Currency Converter</b> is a privacy-focused currency converter app that works offline. Sync exchange rates from the free Frankfurter API and convert currencies even when you're not connected to the internet.<br><br>
<b>Features:</b><br>
<ul>
  <li>Works completely offline</li>
  <li>Supports 160+ currencies</li>
  <li>Automatic exchange rate sync</li>
  <li>Recent conversion history</li>
  <li>Material Design 3 interface</li>
</ul>
This app is free and open source (AGPLv3).
